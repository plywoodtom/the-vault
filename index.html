The Concept

Five eras. One Vault.

Guests travel through five themed era neighborhoods — 40 to 45 custom challenge games — wrapped around a full scratch kitchen and bar. Grab a wristband and play all-access, or just come to eat and drink. A signature feature auto-pauses your game clock while you eat, so a meal never costs you play time. Nobody else does this.

Why It Fits Your District

A year-round anchor, not a weekend novelty.

  • Fills the quiet nights. An experiential anchor that draws traffic on non-game nights and keeps people on-site for hours.
  • Feeds off the stadium. Captures the crowd before and after matches — dinner, drinks, games — instead of letting them leave the district.
  • A weekday engine. Monday–Wednesday corporate buyouts, team-building and private parties fill the window that kills most restaurants.
  • Evergreen & shareable. Themed eras drive repeat visits, expand over time, and every zone is a photo moment — free marketing and foot traffic for the whole district.
