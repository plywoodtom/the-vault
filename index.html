The Concept

Five eras. One vault.

Guests travel through five themed era neighborhoods — 40 to 45 custom challenge games — wrapped around a full scratch kitchen and bar. Grab a wristband and play all-access, or just come to eat and drink. A signature feature auto-pauses your game clock while you eat, so a meal never costs you play time. Nobody else does this.

Why It Fits the Palo District

The anchor a Theme Park District was built for.

  • Built for a Theme Park District. That designation exists to build an entertainment-centric district — and a catalytic, high-caliber interactive attraction is exactly what it calls for. We help deliver the vision, not just fill a box.
  • Feeds the captive crowds. 600 hotel rooms of youth-soccer tournament families, sports-medicine researchers, and business travelers — plus stadium fans — are exactly our customer. After a game or a conference, they want group-friendly active fun.
  • Chef-driven dining that fills the off-hours. A real scratch kitchen and craft bar — and because people come to play, we pull crowds well beyond the standard lunch-and-dinner windows.
  • Tech-forward, not a retro arcade. A digitally-tracked, gamified experience on proprietary RFID, scoring, and booking tech — an anchor as next-generation as a district building an AI venture studio.
  • Evergreen & shareable. Themed eras drive repeat visits, expand over time, and every zone is a photo moment — free marketing and foot traffic for the whole district.
